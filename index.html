PacMan
The PacMan project produces a PacMan with each click of a button. Once you have all of the PacMans you want on the screen, click another button to get them moving!
Eye Movement
The Eye Movement project displays two eyes on the screen which follows your mouse's every move.
Subway
The Subway project uses MapBox and coordinates from the New York City subway system to move a marker across every subway stop in NYC.
